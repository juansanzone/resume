My first contact with programming was when I was thirteen years old. Since that moment, I have been always programming or thinking about doing it and developing new ideas. I became passionate about programming languages, technology and Internet. I obtained multiple programming languages certificates from UTN (Universidad Tecnologica Nacional) I have worked in systems development area for over ten years. Some of the wellknown companies I worked for were Indra, Telecom, Globant Disney, OLX and Letgo. I am currently working for Mercadolibre & Mercadopago Apps as a iOS Sr. Developer. For the last three years I have been full dedicated to the development of iOS applications. I became really strong in iOS apps, specially in user experience interfaces and the end to end development of User-centered mobile products. My free time is also invested in programming. I have several personal ongoing projects like Pasting.io, Planeta Boca App, Waterink and Ridit App. You can check them in mobile portfolio section.
